About YG University

YG University is one of the oldest universities in India, located in the vibrant city of Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We are committed to providing high-quality education to students from all walks of life, and our global presence is a testament to our dedication to academic excellence.

Our university offers a wide range of courses, from intermediate to post-graduation, in various fields such as computer science, engineering, business, and more. Our faculty is comprised of experienced professionals who are passionate about teaching and mentoring students.

At YG University, we believe in providing a holistic education that goes beyond the classroom. Our state-of-the-art facilities, including a world-class library, spacious playgrounds, and tasty and healthy cafeterias, ensure that students have everything they need to succeed in their academic and personal lives.

We are proud of our diverse and inclusive community, and we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all students. Our alumni have gone on to achieve great things in their chosen fields, and we are committed to helping our current students do the same.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide high-quality education to students from all walks of life, and to empower them to become leaders in their chosen fields. We aim to create a diverse and inclusive community where students can learn, grow, and thrive.

Our vision is to be a leading university in India and around the world, known for our academic excellence, innovative research, and commitment to social responsibility.

Our History

YG University was founded in 1956 as a small college in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Over the years, we have grown into a large and prestigious university, with campuses in various parts of the world. Our history is a testament to our commitment to academic excellence and our dedication to providing high-quality education to students from all walks of life.
